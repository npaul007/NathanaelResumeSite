About Me

I am an aspiring software developer currently studying Computer Science. Before I had decided to choose Computer Science as my major, the idea of being a Software Engineer seemed very boring to me.

It was only until I opened my eyes that I realized that software has an impact on everything in our society today, that I fell in love with this career path. Software Engineering allows people to make a huge difference in the world, at the press of a button.

Because of this fact, I want to use this outlet in order to help improve the quality of life of every individual alive, and to ultimately make the world a better place.
